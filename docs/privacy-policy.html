Privacy Policy for Skillful Chrome Extension

Information Collection and Use

The Skillful extension collects and stores the following information locally in your browser:

  • Your resume text that you provide
  • Your OpenAI API key
  • Temporary screenshots of job listings you choose to analyze

Data Storage

All user data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. We do not maintain any servers or databases to store your information.

Data Transmission

The extension transmits the following data to OpenAI's API:

  • Your resume text (only during analysis)
  • Screenshots of job listings you choose to analyze

This transmission is necessary for the core functionality of comparing your resume with job listings.

Third-Party Services

We use OpenAI's API for analyzing job listings. Please refer to OpenAI's Privacy Policy for information about how they handle data.

Data Retention

Your data remains in your browser until you:

  • Update your resume
  • Change your API key
  • Uninstall the extension
  • Clear your browser data
